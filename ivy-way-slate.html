Key Features

This popular slate sign would look perfect whether on a country cottage or a contemporary house, featuring a delicate embellishment of ivy way along the bottom of the slate.

The white text is digitally UV printed against the grey slate material, so it contrasts nicely whilst also making it clear and easy to read. It also ensures that the text is completely weatherproof and will not fade.
